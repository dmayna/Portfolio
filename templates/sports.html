The Raiders

Las Vegas' new spotlight should shine bright this fall. The raiders over achieved las season with a 6-4 record 10 weeks into the season wih arguably the hardest schedule the NFL has ever seen. Sadly the Raiders hit a rookie wall last year and were plagued my injuries to just finish their last 6 games with a 1-5 record. The Raiders still were able to finish with a top 10 offense with no vertical threat at the wide receivers position due to Antonio Brown being cut days before the start of the season. Josh Jacobs had an outstanding year with over 1,100 yards usually having to work hard in crowded boxes. Darren Waller also had 90 receptions and over 1,100 yards and is an extremely underrated Tight End. This year Jacobs and Waller should have plenty of more room to work with since the Raiders added Henry Ruggs III with the 12th overall pick in the 2020 draft. Ruggs ran a blazing 4.27 and is now Jon Gruden's version of Tyreek Hill. This upgrade will benefit our offense entirely but I think the most importnt thing the Raiders did this offseason was address the defense, our linebacker core in Specifically. This is the first time in decades that the Raiders will have two stud linebackers who can cover.We're building a culture with the Raiders and Mike Mayock will prove again he is the best day 3 drafter in the league.

Los Angeles Lakers

The Los Angeles lakers are great again since Kobe's departure and it's terrible he wasn't able to see this season finish out and the see future direction of the Organization. That being said, basketball was never my favorite sport, but Kobe Bryant was always my favorite athlete becuase he made the athlete in me always dig a little deeper. The acquisition of Lebron two years ago immediately made the Lakers a contender and it was sad not seeing them make the playoffs lst year due to Lebron's injury on Christmas and all of the trade rumors. Thus year was different, Lebron attracted the talent and we received great new players in free ageny and trade including Anthony Davis and Danny Green. The pick and roll with Lebron and Davis is nearly unstopable especially with a knock down shooter like Danny Green waiting to be left open.
